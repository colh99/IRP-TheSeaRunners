Karlsson

"Why're you coming on the escape?"
"Maybe to see how it'll be" (62).

Karlsson lives up to the supposed stereotype of his homeland Småland, being taciturn and keeping most thought to himself. As such, he flies under the radar and his personal motivations are unclear, however he trusts Melander's planning. Melander sees the value in Karlsson's canoeing skill, but he is also an adequate woodsman, so he can help hunt for additional food if needed.

Physical Description:

“There always was about this Karlsson a calm just short of chill. He was a Smålander, and that ilk were known to have in them whatever stone God had left over after He filled their fields with it” (19).

Skillset:

“A time, he had noticed Karlsson canoeing in across Sitka Sound here, back from a day’s hunting. Karlsson’s thrifty strokes went beyond steady. Tireless, in a neat-handed, workaday fashion. The regularity of a small millwheel, Melander had been put in mind of as he watched Karlsson paddle” (15).

To escape, the group stole a canoe from a nearby Tlingit village.
