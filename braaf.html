Braaf

“‘My fingers should ever see the day they’re fast as your tongue, Melander’” (23).

Braaf grew up on the streets of Stockholm, and has lived off of his expertise in thievery and pickpocketing. Because of this, he is able to contribute to the escape by acquiring the supplies needed for the long trip: food, weapons, sail, rowing oars, and more. He is perhaps the most eager of the four to leave New Archangel. He finds it difficult to wait in the days leading up to their planned escape.

Physical Description:

“At twenty, he displayed the round ruddy face of a farmboy―an apple of a face―and in talking with you lofted his gaze with innocent interest just above your eyes, as if considerately measuring you for a hat” (22).

Skillset:

“Put it simply, stealing was in Braaf like blood and breath…Alaska he had veered to because after a steady growth of talent from beggary to picking pockets to thievery the other destination imminently beckoning to him was fängelse: prison.” (21).
